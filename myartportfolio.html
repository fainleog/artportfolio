the artwork !!

#1 tuulemõrv and #2 tähed were created together in late 2024 for the mark-making unit. Materials used were stonehenge paper, black ink as well as pink ink, a brush, and a knife for #1 tuulemõrv which was stabbed multiple times with it.

This album cover was created in late 2024 in the album cover unit in media. Procreate was used to create it and my main reference was a picture I took of Kirkjufell (by grundarfjörður) when I was in Iceland in 2023. The title “Í ljósinu" is just Icelandic for “In the light”. I had no idea what to call it so I just settled on that as it looked nice enough, still feels a bit overly religious though which is not at all what I was going for.

This movie poster was made in Adobe Illustrator towards the start of the Fall term in 2024 in media class. I was mainly inspired by the Irish film An Cailín Ciúin and the Irish novel Cré na Cille, from which the movie’s name is a play on. The name means “Graveyard Cat” as opposed to “Graveyard Clay” being the name of the novel inspiring the name. I could give some elaborate made up reason as to why this is but honestly, it's just because I like cats as you may be able to tell from this website.

This piece was made in 2024 for our mark making unit. It took a while for me to figure out what exactly I wanted to make. But, in the end I decided I’d make a cat stamp out of an eraser and then write the word cat in roughly 40 different languages in the shape of the cat. I wrote with a fineliner and used stonehenge paper. Overall It was a fun project and I think that despite its simplicity it is rather nice.

Finally, here is my “brand” poster for my canned drink company, made in 2025 for media in our logo unit. I was mostly inspired by soviet era logos and decided to keep everything simple whilst remaining recogniseable and distinct. The cans themselves have writing in Russian and Ukrainian, as would be common in the soviet union and in this case the Ukrainian SSR. The name of the brand is Напиток Народа or “Drink of the People” and the slogan Здесъ для тебя means “Here for you”, as in it being a readily available drink. The “flavours” themselves were differentiated by both a stripe on the can and the pattern at the bottom giving them each a simple yet distinct look.
